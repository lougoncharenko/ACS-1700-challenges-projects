Training Regimen for Jujitsu

Importance of stretching and Mobility

Each training session begins with stretching and mobility training. Before starting Jujitsu, I never used to stretch or focus on mobility training. Once I started grappling, I quickly noticed that without the mobility and fexibility, I was at a disadvantage. Since practicing the sport, my flexibility has increased due to the specific movements I have been incorportating. Flexibility and stretching play an important role Brazilian Jujitsu for the following reasons:

  • Helps you control your body to perform techniques precise & quick.
  • Helps prevent injuries
  • Improves physical performance
  • Reduces muscular and joint pain
  • Helps relax the body
  • Provides more blood and nutrients to the muscles

Strength Training and Cardio

I currently train between 4-5 days a week at Jujitsu. When I'm not at my training gym, I supplement with cardio such as biking, jump rope, battle ropes, boxing, and jogging for conditioning. I put myself through conditioning workouts involving kettlebells and body weight workouts to improve my athleticism. I also lift weights to build strength 5 times per week following a Push, Pull, and Legs split
